ABOUT US

2014年初，由2位行业资深经纪人召集起上海地区最顶尖的模特管理团队，积极获取最广泛的国际模特资源，成立了FAME Models国际模特经纪公司。

公司成立至今，已经签下来自巴西、俄罗斯、西班牙、德国等地的15位专属模特。也正在极力筹备，更符合中国市场需求的日本、韩国等地的亚洲面孔。为客户带来更多样化的选择是公司始终贯彻的服务理念。

FAME Models一心致力于为所有潮流时尚及媒体品牌送上最诚挚的服务和最优质的模特资源。

Fame Models Management is a professional/full-service model management based in Shanghai. As director of FAME, Dannis Yu has over 6 years experience in model management skill and agent, excellent network of industry. We provide talent faces for commercial shooting, magazines, fashion show, TV commercial, exhibits,and fashion activities.We have established a good cooperation with number of international and domestic first-class photographers, makeup artists, stylists, designers and have many years’experiences in the fashion show and all types of promotion activities, we also provide various types of activity management service to our clients.

We regularly select excellent models for China market from the agencies in France, Italy, US,UK, Japan, Brazil, Russia, Germany, Canada and Australia etc. To provide sufficient quantity and high quality, we irregularly our models in China so as to ensure new faces appear in China from time to time.

We are dedicated to help clients building brand image and competitiveness, as well as providing a best brokerage service for models from the world. We sincerely invite you to inquire and cooperation. We are all at your service for your career development.
